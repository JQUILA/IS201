About Me

This is what I do.

I am a current sophomore at Brigham Young University. Originally from Mexico, I have lived in Arizona since I was 6 and have always loved to go camping.

Some of my favorite adventures have included hiking the Grand Canyon, Zion National Park, and any mountain range close by.

A few places on my list to visit include: Yosemite, Mt. Timpanogos, Mt. Elbert, and Machu Pichu. Hiking is always more fun (and safe I guess) with a friend so let me know if you're intersted in joining me!
